About

I'm an Astronomer from Chile, where the night sky looks better than anywhere else in the world. I obtained my Ph.D. from The University of Tokyo, and my main interest are variable stars and their chemical characterization. Observer, experienced spectroscopist, mainly in the infrared regime. I have spent the last years looking for different and elusive chemical elements in the atmosphere of Cepheids. Now interested into applying the knowledge we gathered using Cepheids on M-dwarf stars, so an improved characterization of the star and its exoplanet(s) is feasible.
